ACCTHPA 2019

ACCTHPA 2019, the sixth International Conference on “ADVANCED COMPUTING AND COMMUNICATION TECHNIQUES FOR HIGH PERFORMANCE APPLICATIONS" is a prime conferences which intends to provide a forum that brings together the International researchers from academia and industry to convene and swap over the ideas and recent research work on the High Performance Applications.

Call for papers

ACCTHPA 2019 invites original papers that move the field of high performance computing forward, be it through the advance of theory, methodology or practice. We invite submissions of high international standard, presenting original work that significantly contributes to research and practice of High Performance Computing in it's broadest sense.

Key dates

Submission deadline for research papers (of variable length, 4-10 pages). All research papers, previously submitted in two categories, long and short, are now in the same category. We are soliciting papers of variable length from 4-10 pages, excluding references. Authors are encouraged to submit papers whose length is proportional to their contributions.

Deadline for all other types of submissions: work in progress papers, posters, demos, design cases, future scenarios, practitioners & industry experiences, workshops, tutorials and doctoral colloquium.
